# RESEARCH NOTES

This folder houses early fragments, rough ideas, conceptual sketches, and working notes.  
This is the compost from which the theory grows.

## Includes
- messy thought fragments  
- partial models  
- raw idea sketches  
- quotes, excerpts, and early comparative notes  

## Does Not Include
- personal trauma logs  
- sensitive information  
- identifiable people or places  
- operator-level cognitive instructions
